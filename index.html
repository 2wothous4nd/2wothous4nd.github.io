The Ghosted

We met online, on league.
She played Yuumi. I played Draven.
She was funny, smart, charming.
She said she was looking for someone to duo with.
We clicked right away.

We mesaged every day, for hours. We shared our hopes, our fears, our secrets.
We made each other laugh, we made each other feel.
We had so much in common, so much chemistry.

We decided to meet in person, after a week of talking.
She said she couldn’t wait to see me, to hold me, to kiss me.
She said she was falling for me, and I felt the same.

We agreed on a date, a time, a place. I was nervous, but excited.
I picked out my best outfit, bought flowers and RP card.
I checked my phone for her confirmation, but there was none.

I waited for her call, her text, her message.

But there was nothing.

I tried to reach her, but she didn’t answer.
I checked her profile, but it was gone.

She ghosted me.
She disappeared from my life, without a word, without a reason.
She broke my heart, without a care, without a remorse.

She was never real.
She was never mine.
She was never there...
